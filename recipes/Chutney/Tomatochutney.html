🍅 Tomato Chutney Recipe 🍅

A delightful, Tomato chutney with a dash of love! 🥰

Ingredients

  • 🍅 4 juicy tomatoes
  • 🧄 2 onion
  • 🌿 A small piece of tangy tamarind
  • 🔥 12 dried red chilies (spicy adjust to taste)
  • 🧂 Salt, as needed

For Seasoning:

  • 🥄 3 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 🌱 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 💛 1 teaspoon urad dal (split black gram)
  • ✨ A pinch of asafoetida (hing)
  • 🍃 A few fresh curry leaves

Steps

  1. Prepare the chutney:
    • First, grind the tomatoes, tamarind, dried red chilies, and salt until the mixture is mostly smooth. Then add the roughly chopped onion and grind again until the mixture is smooth but still slightly coarse.
  2. Seasoning:
    • Heat 3 tablespoons of oil in a pan.
    • Add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
    • Add urad dal, asafoetida (hing), and curry leaves. Sauté until the dal is golden brown and aromatic.
  3. Cook the chutney:
    • Add the Onion-tomato paste to the pan with the seasoning.
    • Simmer over medium heat for about 10 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the oil separates and the chutney thickens.
🌟 Tip: Add a tablespoon of tomato paste with the fresh tomatoes for a deeper flavor and a rich color.
💕 Enjoy this delicious chutney with idlis, dosas, or even spread on toast for a fiery kick! 💕
