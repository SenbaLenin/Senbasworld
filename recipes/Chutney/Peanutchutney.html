Peanut Chutney Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup roasted peanuts
  • 4â€“5 dried red chilies (adjust to taste)
  • 1 tsp oil (for frying chilies)
  • A small piece of tamarind
  • 1-inch piece of ginger
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • Salt, as needed
  • Optional: 2 tbsp grated coconut (for creaminess)

For seasoning:

  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 tsp urad dal (split black gram)
  • A pinch of asafoetida (hing)
  • A few fresh curry leaves

Steps

  1. Prepare the chutney:
    • Heat 1 tsp of oil in a pan and fry the dried red chilies until aromatic.
    • Grind the roasted peanuts, fried chilies, tamarind, ginger, garlic, salt, and optional coconut into a smooth paste, adding water as needed.
  2. Seasoning:
    • Heat 2 tbsp of oil in a pan.
    • Add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
    • Add urad dal, hing, and curry leaves. SautÃ© until the dal turns golden brown.
    • Pour this seasoning over the chutney and mix well.
Tip: For a smoky twist, dry roast the peanuts and chilies together before grinding. It adds a deep, earthy flavor!
