🌿 Mint-Coriander Chutney

Ingredients

  • 1 cup fresh coriander leaves (cilantro)
  • 1 cup fresh mint leaves
  • ½ cup fresh spinach
  • 1–2 green chilies (to taste)
  • ½ inch ginger, peeled
  • 1–2 garlic cloves (optional)
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds (jeera)
  • 2 tbsp roasted peanuts
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp sugar 🍬
  • Salt to taste
  • A splash of water (as needed for blending)

Instructions

  1. Rinse all greens thoroughly and pat dry.
  2. Place all ingredients in a blender or food processor.
  3. Blend until smooth, adding water gradually to reach desired consistency.
  4. Taste and adjust seasoning—more lemon for tang, more chilies for heat.

Tips

  • For extra zing, add a pinch of chaat masala.
  • Store in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days.
💕Use it as a dip, sandwich spread, or drizzle for grilled dishes.UIt also pairs beautifully as a side with Indian favorites like pesarattu, dosa, idli, samosa—and the list keeps growing with every bite.💕
