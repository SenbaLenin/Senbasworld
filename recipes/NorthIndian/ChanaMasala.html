Chana Masala

Spicy and tangy North Indian chickpea curry.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked channa (chickpeas)
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 2 tsp ginger garlic paste
  • 2 tsp channa masala powder
  • 1 tsp cumin seed
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Jaggery 1 tsp
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat oil, add cumin seeds, sauté onions until golden.
  2. Add ginger garlic paste, cook for 1 min.
  3. Add tomatoes, cook until oil separates.
  4. Add spices, then channa add one finely chapped green chili and tamarind pulp one tsp which is optional. Simmer 10 min.
  5. Add 1 tsp jagery then Simmer 2 to 3 min.
  6. Garnish with coriander. Serve hot with rice or roti.

Notes

  • No chenna masala don't worry, Add 1tsp coriender powder, 1/2 tsp cumin powder and 1/2 tsp garam masala.
  • For richer flavor, add a little kasuri methi.
  • For richer color, add tomota paste two spoon while adding tomoto.
  • Tip: To save time, soak them in hot water for 10 minutes, then pressure cook in the Instant Pot for 15 minutes. This method shortens the cooking time and lets you prepare the dish spontaneously—no advance planning needed.
