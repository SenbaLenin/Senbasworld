🌶️ Dal Makhani Recipe

A creamy, comforting Indian lentil dish

🌾 Ingredients

  • ¼ cup whole green moong dal
  • ¼ cup whole black gram (urad dal)
  • 2 tbsp split channa dal
  • 2 tbsp black-eyed beans (lobia)
  • 2 cups water (for pressure cooking)
  • 2 tbsp butter (or oil)
  • 1 tsp ghee (optional)
  • 1 bay leaf, 1 small cinnamon stick, 2 cloves
  • ½ tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 medium tomato, pureed + 1 tsp tomato paste (optional)
  • ½ tsp red chili powder
  • ½ tsp coriander powder
  • ¼ tsp cumin & garam masala powder
  • ¼ tsp turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp heavy cream (optional)
  • Chopped cilantro for garnish

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Cook the Dals: Soak all dals overnight. Pressure cook with water and salt until soft.
  2. Prepare the Gravy: Sauté spices in butter/ghee. Add onions until golden, then ginger-garlic paste. Add tomatoes & cook till thick.
  3. Spice it Up: Stir in chili, coriander, cumin, turmeric, garam masala, and salt.
  4. Combine: Add cooked dals. Adjust consistency with water. Simmer for 10–12 minutes.
  5. Finish: Stir in cream (optional). Garnish with cilantro.
