How to Make protein ball

Ingredients

  • ½ cup Almonds
  • ½ cup Cashews
  • ½ cup Peanuts
  • ½ cup Walnuts
  • ¼ cup Pumpkin Seeds
  • 2 tbsp White Sesame Seeds
  • ¾ cup Jaggery
  • 1 cup Dates (pitted)
  • ½ tsp Cardamom (Elachi)
  • A few strands of Saffron-optional
  • 1–2 tbsp Ghee- optional

Instructions

  • Dry roast all nuts, seeds, and sesame until aromatic. Let cool.
  • Pulse grind into a coarse powder.
  • Blend dates, jaggery, cardamom, and saffron into a sticky paste.
  • Mix paste with nut-seed powder. Add ghee if desired.
  • Shape into small balls using your palms.
  • Store in an airtight container. Enjoy anytime!

Tip: For a festive touch, roll the ladoos in crushed pistachios or coconut flakes.
