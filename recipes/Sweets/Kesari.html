🍊 Kesari Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup coarse rava (semolina)
  • 3 cups water
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1 pinch kesari color (or saffron)
  • 1 tsp milk or water to mix color
  • 2–3 tsp ghee
  • 1–2 tbsp cashews
  • 1–2 tbsp raisins
  • ½ tsp cardamom powder

Instructions:

  1. Dry roast rava until aromatic.
  2. Boil 3 cups water and stir in roasted rava to avoid lumps.
  3. Cook until thick and rava is cooked.
  4. Gradually add sugar and cook for 5 minutes more.
  5. Mix kesari color with milk/water and stir in.
  6. Add cardamom powder.
  7. Fry cashews and raisins in ghee, then mix into Kesari.
  8. Serve warm and enjoy!

🌈 Variation Tip: Fruity Twist

  • Pineapple Kesari 🍍: Add ½ cup chopped or crushed pineapple (fresh or canned) while cooking the rava. Reduce water slightly to adjust for pineapple juice.You can also mix in a spoonful of pineapple essence for a richer aroma.
  • Mango Kesari 🥭: - Mango Kesari 🥭: Stir in ½ cup mango pulp after the sugar is added. Alphonso mangoes give the best color and flavor.Optional: Add a few saffron strands soaked in warm milk to enhance both taste and color.

💛 Family Tip

Kesari is my Family favorite dish, Learned from my mother-in-law. The secret to enriching its flavor is adding ghee at the end—this not only enhances the aroma but also amplifies the taste while using less ghee.
