Chocolate Cake

Home About

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix dry ingredients in a bowl.
  3. Add eggs, milk, and oil. Mix until smooth.
  4. Pour into a greased baking pan.
  5. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  6. Let cool, then enjoy!
