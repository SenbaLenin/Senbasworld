Sweet Pongal Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup raw rice
  • ¼ cup yellow moong dal
  • 1 cup grated jaggery (adjust to taste)
  • 2–3 cups water
  • ½ cup milk
  • 1–2 tsp ghee
  • 10–12 cashew nuts
  • 8–10 raisins
  • ½ tsp cardamom (elachi) powder

Instructions

  1. Cook rice and moong dal together in water until soft and mushy.
  2. Melt jaggery with a little water and strain to remove impurities.
  3. Mix jaggery syrup into the rice-dal blend and simmer gently.
  4. Add milk and cook for another 5 minutes until thickened.
  5. Fry cashews and raisins in ghee until golden, then stir in.
  6. Sprinkle cardamom powder and serve warm.
