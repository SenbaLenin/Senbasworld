🌶️ Garlic Peanut Spice Mix for Rice

A bold South Indian-style accompaniment, perfect with hot rice and a touch of ghee or sesame oil.

📝 Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp chana dal
  • 1 tbsp urad dal
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds (jeera)
  • 1 sprig curry leaves
  • A small piece of tamarind
  • 15 garlic cloves
  • 1 tbsp oil (for sautéing)
  • 2 tbsp roasted peanuts
  • 2 tsp sambar powder
  • A pinch of asafoetida (hing)
  • Salt to taste
  • Ghee or sesame oil (for serving)

🔥 Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan. Add chana dal, urad dal, cumin seeds, curry leaves, tamarind, and garlic. Fry on medium heat until the dals are golden and aromatic and add roasted peanuts.
  2. Turn off the heat and mix sambar powder, asafoetida, and salt stir well.
  3. Once slightly cooled, grind the mixture coarsely—not too smooth—to retain some crunch and texture.
  4. Spoon the mix over hot rice and top with a generous drizzle of ghee or sesame oil.
