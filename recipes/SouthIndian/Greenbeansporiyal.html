🍲 Green Beans Poriyal

Serves: 3–4
Prep Time: 10 mins
Cook Time: 15–20 mins

🧄 Ingredients

  • 250g green beans (finely chopped or French cut)
  • 1 tbsp oil (coconut or vegetable)
  • 1 tsp urad dal
  • 1 tsp chana dal
  • 1–2 green or dried red chilies (optional)
  • 1 small onion (finely chopped)
  • ¼ tsp turmeric powder
  • ½ tsp chili powder (or sambar powder)
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tsp soaked or cooked mung dal (tender)
  • 2–3 tbsp grated fresh coconut
  • Fresh cilantro (chopped, for garnish)

🍳 Instructions

  1. Wash and trim the green beans. Chop them finely or use French-cut style.
  2. Heat oil in a pan. Add urad dal and chana dal. Sauté until golden brown.
  3. Add chilies and chopped onion. Sauté until onions turn soft and translucent.
  4. Add green beans, turmeric, chili powder, and salt. Mix well.
  5. Cover and cook on low heat for 8–10 minutes, stirring occasionally. Sprinkle water if needed.
  6. Stir in the soaked or cooked mung dal. Cook for 1–2 minutes.
  7. Add grated coconut and chopped cilantro. Mix and cook for another minute.
  8. Turn off the heat and serve warm with rice and sambar or rasam.
