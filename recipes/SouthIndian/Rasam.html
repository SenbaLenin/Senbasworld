Authentic Rasam Recipe

Rasam Powder

  • 1.5 tsp coriander seeds
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds (jeera)
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1 dried red chili

Grind coarsely to make the rasam powder.

Main Ingredients

  • 1 tsp crushed garlic cloves
  • 1 small tomato, chopped
  • A few coriander leaves (cilantro)
  • 2.5 cups water
  • Tamarind juice (to taste)
  • Dal water (from cooked lentils)
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Pinch of turmeric powder

Seasoning

  • 1 tsp oil
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds (jeera)
  • 1 small green chili, slit
  • A few curry leaves
  • A pinch of asafoetida (Hing)

Instructions

  1. Make the Rasam:
    In a vessel, gently mix the rasam powder, garlic, tomato, coriander leaves, tamarind juice, water, dal water, turmeric powder, and salt. Simmer on medium heat for 5 minutes, until it just begins to bubble. Remove from heat.
  2. Prepare the Seasoning:
    In a small pan, heat oil. Add mustard seeds and let them crackle, then add cumin seeds, green chili, curry leaves, and asafoetida. Sauté briefly until aromatic.
  3. Combine & Serve:
    Pour the fragrant seasoning into the hot rasam and give it a gentle stir. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve piping hot.

Tip: This rasam is delicious even without dal water. Adjust tanginess and spice to your taste!
