Spicy South Indian Beetroot Poriyal

Prep: 10 min  |  Cook: 15 min  |  Serves: 2–3

Ingredients

  • 1–2 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 tsp urad dal
  • 1 tsp split channa dal
  • A few curry leaves
  • Green chilies (slit, to taste)
  • one small onion
  • 1–2 cups shredded beetroot
  • 2 tsp grated coconut
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds and let them pop.
  2. Stir in urad dal, channa dal, curry leaves, onion ,and green chilies. Sauté until golden and aromatic.
  3. Add shredded beetroot and cook until tender, stirring occasionally.
  4. Mix in grated coconut and simmer briefly to blend flavors.
  5. Garnish with cilantro and serve warm.
Serving Tip: This vibrant dish pairs wonderfully with steamed rice, rasam, or tucked into warm chapatis.
