Keerai Kootu (Tamil-style)

🧾 Ingredients

  • 2 cups chopped Spinach/keerai
  • ½ cup Mung dal ( yellow split lentils), boiled
  • ¼ tsp turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • ½ to 1 tsp red chili powder
  • ¼ cup grated coconut
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds (jeera)
  • A few curry leaves
  • Tomoto (Optional)

🍳 Instructions

  1. Grind coconut, cumin seeds, and curry leaves into a smooth paste.
  2. Boil chopped Spinach with turmeric, chili powder, and salt until soft.
  3. Add the cooked Mung dal (pasi parupu), tomoto and coconut paste to the Spinach. Simmer for 5–7 minutes.
  4. Serve hot with rice. Enjoy!
