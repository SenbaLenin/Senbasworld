South Indian Sambar Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp oil (sesame or vegetable)
  • ½ tsp mustard seeds
  • ½ tsp urad dal
  • ½ tsp cumin seeds (jeera)
  • 1 sprig curry leaves
  • A pinch of asafoetida (hing)
  • 1 green chili, slit
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • Salt to taste
  • 1½ tsp sambar powder
  • ¼ tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 cup cooked toor dal
  • 1 tbsp tamarind water
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Instructions

  • Heat oil, add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
  • Add urad dal, cumin, curry leaves, asafoetida, and chili. Sauté briefly.
  • Add onions, cook until translucent. Add veggies, tomato, salt, sambar powder, turmeric. Sauté 1 min.
  • Pour in cooked toor dal and 2 cups of water. Simmer 10 minutes.
  • Stir in tamarind water, simmer 5 more minutes.
  • Garnish with coriander and serve hot.
🌾 A classic from South India — perfect with rice, idli, dosa, or vada!
