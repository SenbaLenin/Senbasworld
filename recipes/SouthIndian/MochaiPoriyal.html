🍲 Mochai Poriyal (Lima Bean Stir-Fry)

📋 Ingredients

  • Lima beans – 2 cups (fresh or frozen)
  • To grind into a paste:
    • Green chilies – 2
    • Cumin seeds – ½ tsp
    • Cinamon sticks 1
    • Grated coconut – 2–3 tbsp
    • Curry leaves – a few
    • Ginger and garlic – optional

    For seasoning:

    • Mustard seeds – ½ tsp
    • Cumin seeds – ½ tsp
    • Urad dal – 1 tsp
    • Onion – optional
    • Curry leaves – a few
    • Turmeric powder – ¼ tsp
    • Salt – to taste
    • Water – a splash
    • Oil – 1 tbsp

    👨‍🍳 Instructions

    1. Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, urad dal, and curry leaves. Sauté until the dal turns golden. Add onion if using and cook until translucent.
    2. Add lima beans and sauté for 2–3 minutes.
    3. Add the ground paste and mix well.
    4. Sprinkle turmeric and salt. Add a 1/4 cup water. Cover and cook on low heat until the beans are soft.
    5. Once cooked, uncover and cook until any remaining moisture evaporates and the mixture is dry.
    6. Serve hot with rice, rasam, or sambar.
