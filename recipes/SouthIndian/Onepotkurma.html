🍛 One-Pot Vegetable Kurma

🧄 Ground Paste

  • Grated coconut – ¼ cup
  • Fennel seeds (sombu) – 1 tsp
  • Green chilies – 2
  • Cinaman – 2 small
  • Water – as needed

🥦 Vegetables

  • Potato, carrot, beetroot – cubed
  • Beans, peas – chopped
  • Onion – sliced (optional)
  • Green chili – slit

🌿 Whole Spices

  • Butter or oil – 1 tbsp
  • Fennel seeds – ½ tsp
  • Cloves – 2
  • Cinnamon stick – 1 small
  • Cashews – 6–8
  • Cardamom (elachi) – 2

🔥 Cooking Steps

  1. Add all vegetables (including onion and green chili if using) to a pot.
  2. Pour in the grounded paste and mix well.
  3. Add water to adjust consistency. Cover and cook until vegetables are tender (about 10–15 minutes).
  4. Add salt to taste and simmer for a few more minutes.
  5. In a separate pan, heat butter or oil. Add fennel seeds, cloves, cinnamon, and cardamom. Let them sizzle.
  6. Pour this seasoning into the cooked curry and stir well.
  7. Garnish with coriander leaves if desired.

🍽 Serve hot with chapati, poori, or rice. Enjoy your meal!
