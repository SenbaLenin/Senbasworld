Aromatic South Indian Spice Blend

Ingredients

  • 20 red chilies
  • 1 cup channa dal
  • 1 cup urad dal
  • One handful curry leaves
  • Asafetida ‚Äì as needed
  • Salt ‚Äì as needed

Instructions

  1. Dry roast red chilies until aromatic.
  2. Dry roast channa dal and urad dal until golden brown.
  3. Dry roast curry leaves until crisp.
  4. Add asafetida and salt to the roasted ingredients.
  5. Grind everything together into a coarse powder.
  6. Store in an airtight container.

Notes

  • Serve with idli or dosa, mixed with sesame oil or ghee.
  • Adjust the heat by modifying the number of red chilies.
  • Keep the texture slightly coarse for added flavor and crunch.
