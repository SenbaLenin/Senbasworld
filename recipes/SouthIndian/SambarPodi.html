Fresh Sambar Podi Recipe

🌿 Ingredients:

  • Coriander seeds – 1 kg
  • Red chili – 1/2 kg
  • Black pepper – 100 g
  • Jeera (Cumin seeds) – 200 g
  • Turmeric – 200 g
  • Toor dal – One handful
  • Channa dal – One handful
  • Curry leaves – One handful
  • Asafoetida – As needed (LG brand, dry roasted)

📝 Instructions:

  1. Grind all the ingredients together into a fine powder.
  2. Store in an airtight container and use as needed.

💡 Notes:

  • Since the ingredients aren't dried, ensure they are fresh and clean before grinding.
  • Store in a dry, airtight container for longer shelf life.
