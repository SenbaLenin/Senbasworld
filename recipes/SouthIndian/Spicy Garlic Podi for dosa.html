🔥Spicy Garlic Podi Blitz – Quick & Easy!

A quick and flavorful chutney powder to elevate your dosa or idli!

🧄 Ingredients

  • 7 or 8 garlic cloves (peeled)
  • 2 tbsp sambar powder
  • 1 tsp salt (adjust to taste)

🍳 Method

  1. Blend garlic, sambar powder, and salt into a powder.
  2. Adjust salt/spice as needed and store in an airtight container.
  3. Pair garlic podi served with sesame oil makes a flavorful side dish for dosa or idli.
