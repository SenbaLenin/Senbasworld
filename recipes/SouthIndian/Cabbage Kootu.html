🥣 Cabage Kootu Recipe

🧾 Ingredients

  • 2 cups chopped cabbage
  • ½ cup channa dal (Bengal gram), soaked and boiled
  • ¼ tsp turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • ½ to 1 tsp red chili powder
  • ¼ cup grated coconut
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds (jeera)
  • A few curry leaves
  • Optional: 1 tbsp rice (for thickening)

🍳 Instructions

  1. Boil chopped cabbage with turmeric, chili powder, and salt until soft.
  2. Grind coconut, cumin seeds, and curry leaves into a smooth paste.
  3. Add the boiled channa dal and coconut paste to the cabbage. Simmer for 5–7 minutes.
  4. If watery, mix 1tsp riceflour with 2 tsp water into a paste and stir in. Simmer 2–3 more minutes.
  5. Serve hot with rice. Enjoy!
