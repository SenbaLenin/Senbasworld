🍽️ Vazhakkai Roast (Raw Banana Fry)

🧄 Ingredients

  • 2 raw bananas (vazhakkai), peeled and sliced
  • ½ tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chilli powder
  • 1 tsp sambar powder (optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil (sunflower, peanut, or coconut oil)
  • ½ tsp mustard seeds
  • ½ tsp urad dal
  • A few curry leaves
  • A pinch of asafoetida (hing)

🔥 Instructions

  1. Peel and slice the raw bananas. Boil in salted water for 3–4 minutes until just tender. Drain and set aside.
  2. Toss the slices with turmeric, chilli powder, sambar powder, and salt.
  3. Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds and let them splutter. Add urad dal, curry leaves, and asafoetida.
  4. Add the spiced banana slices. Roast on low flame, flipping occasionally, until golden and crisp.
  5. Serve hot with rice and dal or curd.
