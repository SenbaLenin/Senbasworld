Ven Pongal
(Khara Pongal – Instant Pot Method)

Ingredients

  • Rice – ½ cup
  • Moong dal (split yellow lentils) – ½ cup
  • Water – 3 cups
  • Milk – ¾ cup (add after pressure release)
  • Salt – 1 tsp (adjust to taste)
  • Ghee – ½ cup
  • Black pepper – 2 tsp
  • Cumin seeds – 1 tsp
  • Ginger – 1 inch, finely chopped
  • Green chilies – 2 (optional)
  • Cashews – 2 tbsp
  • Asafoetida (hing) – a pinch
  • Curry leaves – few

Method

1. Roast the Dal: Turn on Instant Pot to sauté mode. Add 1 tsp ghee and roast moong dal until aromatic.
2. Cook Rice & Dal: Add rice, 3 cups water, and salt. Cancel sauté mode. Close the lid and pressure cook on high for 8 minutes. Let the pressure release naturally.
3. Add Milk: Once pressure is released, open the lid and stir in warm milk. Mix well to get a creamy consistency.
4. Prepare Tempering: In a separate pan, heat ghee. Splutter pepper and cumin seeds. Add ginger, green chilies, and cashews. Roast until golden. Add curry leaves and hing.
5. Combine: Pour the tempering into the Instant Pot. Mix gently. Adjust consistency with hot water or more milk if needed.
6. Serve Hot: Garnish with extra ghee and fried cashews. Serve with coconut chutney or sambar.

Enjoy your creamy and comforting Ven Pongal made in the Instant Pot!
