🍅 Tomato Thambuli

Ingredients

  • 1 medium ripe tomato – chopped
  • 1/4 cup grated coconut
  • 1 green chilli
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 4–5 black peppercorns
  • Small piece of ginger (optional)
  • 1 cup fresh curd
  • Salt – to taste

Preparation

  1. Heat 1/2 tsp ghee and sauté chopped tomato for 2–3 minutes until soft.
  2. Grind tomato, coconut, cumin, pepper, chilli, and little water into a smooth paste.
  3. Mix the paste with curd and add salt.

Seasoning (Tadka)

  1. Heat 1 tsp ghee.
  2. Add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
  3. Add curry leaves and fry until aromatic.
  4. Pour the seasoning over the thambuli and mix gently.

Serve chilled with hot rice. Perfect for summer and digestion.
