🌶️🍅 Spicy Tomoto Pickle 🍅 🌶️

Ingredients

  • Semi-ripe tomatoes – chopped (2 cups)
  • Carrots – peeled and thinly sliced (1 cup)
  • Garlic cloves – peeled and halved (½ cup)
  • Jalapeños – sliced (½ cup)
  • Tomato paste – 1 small can (about 6 oz)
  • Cooking oil (any neutral oil) – 3 tbsp
  • Red chilli powder – 2 tsp (adjust to taste)
  • Turmeric powder – ½ tsp
  • Salt – to taste
  • Mustard seeds – 1 tsp
  • Urad dal – ½ tsp
  • Curry leaves – a handful
  • Methi (fenugreek) powder – ¼ tsp
  • Asafoetida – a pinch

Preparation

  1. Heat oil in a pan and sauté semi-ripe tomatoes, carrots, garlic, and jalapeños until slightly soft.
  2. Add tomato paste and cook for 2–3 minutes until well blended and thick.
  3. Then add red chilli powder, turmeric, and salt. Mix well and cook until the raw smell fades.
  4. Turn off the heat and set aside.

Seasoning (Tadka)

  1. Heat a little oil in a separate pan.
  2. Add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
  3. Add urad dal and sauté until golden brown.
  4. Add curry leaves, asafoetida, and methi powder. Stir briefly.
  5. Pour the seasoning over the cooked vegetable mixture and mix thoroughly.

Let the pickle cool completely before storing in a clean jar. This bold, tangy mix is perfect with rice, rotis, or even as a spicy sandwich spread!
