Thayir Sadham Recipe

Thayir Sadham (Curd Rice)

Ingredients (Serves 2)

  • Cooked rice – 1 cup
  • Curd – 1.5 cups
  • Milk – ¼ cup (optional)
  • Salt – to taste
  • Grated carrot – 2 tbsp (optional)
  • Chopped cucumber – 2 tbsp (optional)
  • Chopped coriander – 1 tsp
  • Pomegranate seeds – for garnish (optional)

For Tempering

  • Oil – 1 tsp
  • Mustard seeds – ½ tsp
  • Cumin seeds – ½ tsp
  • Chana dal – 1 tsp
  • Urad dal – 1 tsp
  • Dried red chili – 1
  • Green chili – 1 (slit)
  • Ginger – ½ tsp (grated)
  • Curry leaves – 1 sprig
  • Hing – a pinch
  • Cashews – 6 to 8 (optional)

Instructions

  1. Cook rice until soft and mash slightly while warm.
  2. Cool completely before adding curd to avoid curdling.
  3. Mix curd, milk (if using), and salt into rice. Stir until creamy.
  4. Add grated carrot, cucumber, and coriander leaves. Mix well.
  5. Heat oil and add tempering ingredients. Sauté until dal turns golden.
  6. Pour tempering over curd rice and mix.
  7. Garnish with pomegranate seeds or roasted cashews.
  8. Serve chilled or at room temperature with pickle or papad.
