Semiya Payasam
(Vermicelli Kheer)

Ingredients

  • Water – 1 cup
  • Thin vermicelli (semiya) – ¾ cup, fried in ghee or get fried semiya from store
  • Milk – 1 cup
  • Condensed milk – 1 can (adjust to taste)
  • Ghee – 1 tbsp
  • Cashew nuts – 10
  • Raisins – 10
  • Cardamom powder – ¼ tsp

Method

1. Boil Water: In a pan, bring 1 cup of water to a boil.
2. Cook Vermicelli: Add the fried semiya and cook until soft and water is mostly absorbed.
3. Add Milk: Pour in milk and condensed milk. Stir and simmer for 5–7 minutes until creamy.
4. Season: In a small pan, heat ghee. Fry cashews until golden, then add raisins and fry until they puff up.
5. Flavor: Add cardamom powder and Mix gently.
6. Serve: Serve warm or chilled, garnished with extra nuts if desired.

A festive favorite that’s quick, creamy, and comforting!
