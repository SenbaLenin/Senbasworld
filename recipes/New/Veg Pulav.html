🍲 Vegetable Pulao

A flavorful and comforting one-pot rice dish made with vegetables, tomato, and optional soya chunks—perfect for lunch or dinner.

🌾 Ingredients

  • 1 cup basmati rice (soaked for 20 minutes)
  • 1½ cups water
  • 2 tbsp ghee or butter (divided)
  • 1 small onion, sliced
  • 1 green chili, slit
  • 1 tsp ginger garlic paste
  • ½ carrot, chopped
  • 3 beans, chopped
  • ¼ cup green peas
  • ½ small potato, diced
  • 1 medium tomato, chopped
  • ¼ cup soya chunks (optional, soaked in hot water for 10 mins)
  • 2 tbsp mint leaves, chopped
  • corriender powder 1 tsp, briyani masala 1 tsp (I like sindhi briyani masala), garam masala
  • Whole spices:
    • 1 bay leaf
    • 1 inch cinnamon
    • 2 cloves
    • 2 cardamoms
    • ½ star anise
    • ½ tsp shahi jeera
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

👩‍🍳 Method

  1. Rinse and soak rice for 20 minutes. Drain and set aside.
  2. Soak soya chunks in hot water for 10 minutes if using. Squeeze and set aside.
  3. In a pressure cooker, heat 1 tbsp ghee or butter or oil. Add whole spices and let them crackle.
  4. Add onions and green chili. Sauté until golden. Add ginger garlic paste and cook until fragrant.
  5. Add chopped vegetables, tomato, mint leaves, and optional soya chunks. Sauté for 2–3 minutes.
  6. Add remaining 1 tbsp ghee or butter. Add drained rice and sauté gently for 2–3 minutes.
  7. Add corriender powder 1 tsp, briyani masala 1 tsp (I like sindhi briyani masala), garam masala1/4 tsp sauté gently for2 minutes
  8. Pour in 1½ cups water and salt. Mix well and close the lid.
  9. As soon as place the whistle, reduce flame to low and cook for exactly 8 minutes. Turn off the heat.
  10. Let pressure release naturally. Open, fluff gently, and garnish with fresh coriander.

🍽️ Serving Suggestions

  • Serve hot with cucumber or onion raita
  • Pair with crispy chips
