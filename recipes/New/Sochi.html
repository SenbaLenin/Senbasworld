Sochi
(Moong Dal Rice Upma – Instant Pot)

Ingredients

  • Oil – 2 tbsp
  • Mustard seeds – 1 tsp
  • Urad dal – 1 tsp
  • Chana dal – 1 tsp
  • Asafoetida (hing) – a pinch
  • Onion – 1 small, finely chopped
  • Ginger – 1 tsp, grated
  • Curry leaves – a few
  • Moong dal – ½ cup (dry roasted)
  • Raw rice – 1 cup (dry roasted)
  • Water – 2 cups
  • Salt – to taste
  • Fresh coconut – 1/4 cup
  • Fresh cilantro – 2 tbsp, chopped (for garnish)

Method

1. Roast Grains: Dry roast moong dal and raw rice separately until aromatic. Set aside.
2. Sauté Seasoning: Turn Instant Pot to sauté mode. Add oil, mustard seeds, urad dal, and chana dal. Let them splutter and turn golden.
3. Add Aromatics: Add a pinch of asafoetida, chopped onion, ginger, and curry leaves. Sauté until onions are soft.
4. Add Grains: Stir in the roasted moong dal and rice. Mix well.
5. Add Water & Salt: Pour in 2 cups of water and add salt to taste.
6. Pressure Cook: Cancel sauté mode. Set Instant Pot to Pressure Cook (Manual) for 8 minutes. Let pressure release naturally for 5 minutes, then quick release.
7. Garnish & Serve: Fluff gently and garnish with fresh cocunut and fresh cilantro. Serve hot with coconut chutney or pickle.

Warm, comforting, and protein-rich—this Sochi is perfect for any meal of the day!
