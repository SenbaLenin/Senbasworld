Thatapayeru Sadham

Ingredients

  • Oil – 2 tbsp
  • Mustard seeds – 1 tsp
  • Urad dal – 1 tsp
  • Chana dal – 1 tsp
  • Asafoetida (hing) – a pinch
  • Onion – 1 small, finely chopped
  • Ginger – 1 tsp, grated
  • Curry leaves – a few
  • Thatapayeru (black-eyed beans) – ½ cup (soaked and cooked separately)
  • Raw rice – 1 cup (dry roasted)
  • Water – 2 cups
  • Salt – to taste
  • Fresh coconut – 1/4 cup
  • Fresh cilantro – 2 tbsp, chopped (for garnish)

Method

1. Cook Beans: Dry roast black-eyed beans and Pressure cook separately with water and a pinch of salt for 10 minutes until soft. Drain and set aside.
2. Roast Rice: Dry roast raw rice until aromatic. Set aside.
3. Sauté Seasoning: Turn Instant Pot to sauté mode. Add oil, mustard seeds, urad dal, and chana dal. Let them splutter and turn golden.
4. Add Aromatics: Add asafoetida, onion, ginger, and curry leaves. Sauté until onions are soft.
5. Add Rice & Beans: Stir in roasted rice and pre-cooked black-eyed beans. Mix well.
6. Add Water & Salt: Pour in 2 cups of water and add salt to taste.
7. Pressure Cook: Cancel sauté mode. Set Instant Pot to Pressure Cook (Manual) for 6 minutes. Let pressure release naturally for 5 minutes, then quick release.
8. Garnish & Serve: Fluff gently and garnish with Fresh coconut and fresh cilantro. Serve warm with chutney or yogurt.

Nutritious and comforting—this Thatapayeru Sadham
