🌿 Curry Leaf Thambuli

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup fresh curry leaves
  • 1/4 cup grated coconut
  • 1 green chilli
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 4–5 peppercorns (optional)
  • 1 cup fresh curd
  • Salt – to taste
  • 1 tsp ghee (for sautéing leaves)

Preparation

  1. Heat 1 tsp ghee and sauté curry leaves for 1–2 minutes until aromatic.
  2. Grind curry leaves, coconut, chilli, cumin, and little water into a smooth paste.
  3. Mix the paste with curd and add salt.

Seasoning (Tadka)

  1. Heat 1 tsp ghee.
  2. Add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
  3. Add curry leaves and fry briefly.
  4. Pour the seasoning over the thambuli and mix.

Aromatic, herbal, and cooling — best served with hot rice.
