🍋 Lemon Pickle

Ingredients

  • Yellow lemons – cleaned and chopped into small pieces
  • Salt – enough to coat the lemon pieces
  • Red chilli powder – to taste
  • Turmeric powder – ½ tsp
  • Cooking oil (sunflower, canola, or any neutral oil) – 2 tbsp
  • Mustard seeds – 1 tsp
  • Urad dal – ½ tsp
  • Curry leaves – a handful
  • Methi (fenugreek) powder – ¼ tsp
  • Asafoetida – a pinch

Preparation

  1. Cut lemons into small pieces and mix with salt.
  2. Store in a clean jar at room temperature for 3 days, stirring once daily.
  3. After 3 days, add red chilli powder and turmeric. Mix well.

Seasoning (Tadka)

  1. Heat oil in a pan.
  2. Add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
  3. Add urad dal and sauté until golden brown.
  4. Add curry leaves fry until aromatic.
  5. Add asafoetida and methi powder; turn off the heat.
  6. Pour the seasoning over the lemon mixture and mix thoroughly.

Let the pickle rest for a few hours before serving. Store in a clean, dry jar and enjoy with rice, roti, or curd dishes!
