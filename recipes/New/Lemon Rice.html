Lemon Rice
(Chitranna)

Ingredients

  • Cooked rice – 3 cups (cooled and fluffed)
  • Oil – 2 tbsp (preferably sesame or sunflower)
  • Mustard seeds – ¾ tsp
  • Chana dal – 1½ tsp
  • Urad dal – 1 tsp
  • Peanuts – ¼ cup
  • Green chilies – 2, slit
  • Ginger – ½ tbsp, finely chopped
  • Curry leaves – 2 sprigs
  • Turmeric powder – ½ tsp
  • Asafoetida (hing) – a pinch
  • Salt – ½ tsp (adjust to taste)
  • Lemon juice – 3 tbsp (freshly squeezed)
  • Cashews – optional, for garnish

Method

1. Prep the Rice: If using freshly cooked rice, cool it completely and fluff with a fork to avoid clumping.
2. Roast Nuts: Heat ½ tbsp oil in a pan. Fry peanuts (and cashews if using) until golden. Remove and set aside for garnish.
3. Tempering: In the same pan, add remaining oil. Add mustard seeds, chana dal, and urad dal. Fry until golden and aromatic.
4. Add Aromatics: Add green chilies, ginger, curry leaves, and hing. Sauté until curry leaves turn crisp.
5. Add Turmeric: Add turmeric powder and immediately turn off the heat.
6. Mix with Lemon: In a large bowl, combine lemon juice and salt. Pour the hot tempering (with turmeric) into this mixture and stir well.
7. Add Rice: Add the cooled rice to the bowl and mix gently until evenly coated with the lemon-seasoning mixture.
8. Serve: Garnish with fresh cilentro and roasted peanuts and cashews. Serve with pickle, or papad.
